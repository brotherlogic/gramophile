# Onboarding Guide

To get started with Gramophile, you need to securely connect your Discogs account. This guide explains how to authorize the application and complete your initial setup.

## Connecting Your Discogs Account

Gramophile uses Discogs' official authorization process to access and update your collection. You do not need to share your Discogs password.

1. **Get Authorization Link**:
   Start the login process in the Gramophile client. The application will generate a secure, official Discogs authorization link.
2. **Authorize the Application**:
   Clicking the link will redirect you to the Discogs website. If you aren't logged into Discogs, you will be prompted to log in. Discogs will ask if you want to allow Gramophile to view and manage your collection and wantlist. Click **Authorize**.
3. **Finish Login**:
   Once authorized, Discogs will redirect you back, and Gramophile will securely link your session. You are now logged in and ready to use Gramophile!

## Initial Sync

When you log in for the first time, Gramophile needs to import your collection:

1. **Background Import**: Gramophile automatically schedules an initial import task. This runs in the background so you don't have to wait or keep the application open.
2. **Page-by-Page Fetch**: The system retrieves your records from Discogs page-by-page. Depending on the size of your collection, this can take anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes.
3. **Ready to Go**: Once the initial sync is complete, your account status is set to active, enabling features like wants tracking and physical shelves planning.
